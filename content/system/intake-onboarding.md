# Intake + Onboarding Flow (10-Day Results Assurance)
- Intake step: Capture business type, locations, urgency, and primary engagement goal (calls, forms, map actions, or qualified on-site actions).
- Onboarding step: Confirm ad account access within 24 hours and validate phone + website availability.
- Budget step: Set minimum daily budget as agreed to support measurable engagement signals.
- Launch step: Campaign goes live and the 10-day window begins.
- Operations note: Days 1-3 stabilization, days 4-10 optimization and traction.
- Assurance review: Day-10 check for engagement results and eligibility; Budget On Us review if needed; optional continuation without penalties.
