# App Wireframe: Flows
- Flow: New lead from website - Step 1 Lead created with source "Website" and consent record; Step 2 auto-assign to intake rep; Step 3 timeline opens with form payload; Step 4 rep clicks "Call now"; Step 5 outcome logged and follow-up scheduled. Security embedded: webhook signature verified; duplicate protection on phone/email; rate-limited form endpoint.
- Flow: Chatbot booked appointment - Step 1 chatbot logs qualification answers; Step 2 appointment slot reserved; Step 3 confirmation sent; Step 4 lead status "Booked"; Step 5 reminder automation. Security embedded: signed bot events, abuse throttling, PII encrypted.
- Flow: Missed call recovery - Step 1 missed call flagged; Step 2 auto SMS goes out; Step 3 lead moved to "Needs callback"; Step 4 rep calls; Step 5 disposition logged. Security embedded: opt-out honored on first reply; SMS templates locked.
- Flow: Lost lead reactivation - Step 1 status to "Nurture"; Step 2 scheduled follow-up sequence; Step 3 human review before re-contact. Security embedded: contact frequency limits, consent check.
- Flow: Assurance onboarding - Step 1 confirm account access within 24 hours; Step 2 verify business phone and website are live; Step 3 select one primary engagement goal; Step 4 confirm minimum daily budget as agreed; Step 5 launch campaign and start day-1 clock; Step 6 daily engagement checks and adjustments; Step 7 day-10 review and eligibility check; Step 8 mark results achieved or trigger Budget On Us review; Step 9 if refund applied, continue with goodwill runway or close cleanly. Security embedded: eligibility checklist locked to managers; refund approvals require dual sign-off.
- Flow: Assurance status in CRM - Step 1 assurance flag added to lead; Step 2 assurance window start/end stamped; Step 3 engagement signals auto-logged; Step 4 alerts at day 7 and day 10; Step 5 refund review task created if no results logged.
